Arts for Life Inc.

Digital style guide, web design and dev for nonprofit in North Carolina
that supports child facing serious illnesses and disabilities through art.

Arts for Life wanted a website that reflected the vibrance of the organization and the people it serves. We thoroughly studied the previous website, talked to staff and users of the website to get a better understanding of who the audience is. We substantially simplified the information architecture of the site and worked through several iterations of wireframes and wired prototypes using UXpin and Invision prior to arriving to any design decisions. The result was a site that expresses the artistic spirit of the Arts for Life, easy to use for parents and those looking to learn more about the organization, as well as extremely simple to maintain and update for organization's staff.
