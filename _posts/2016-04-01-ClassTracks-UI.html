ClassTracks™ UI/UX Design

UI/UX and product design for Baltimore based education tech startup ClassTracks Inc.

My final execution for the client was the initial ui design of their web application that would be in the hand of foreign language teachers and students, as well as school administrators. Since I was designing this from scratch without alot of information to go on, I had to do a considerable amount of research, first I needed to understand the basic concepts of classroom design from USC Rossier School of Education. While I was designing a digital product, it was important to understand what works in the physical environment of a classroom and then figure out how to translate that most effectively in the digital realm.

  •

In doing the initial design research and concepts, I also created a design system to streamline the design-to-developement process.
