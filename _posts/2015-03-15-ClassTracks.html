ClassTracks™

Identity and brand design for Washington D.C. based education tech startup.

Spent a month studying the company and its product, interviewing the founders and going through multiple style and story board sessions to get a good grasp on identity. The month long process led us to this identity - bright, bold, simple and to the point. Knowing that this was a very young startup, I wanted to provide them a brand and identity that would grow together with the company.
