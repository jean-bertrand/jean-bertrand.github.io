Whole Foods Market™

Marketing design for Whole Foods Market Global HQ

Slide 1. Healthy Eating Meal Plans

Designed and developed to support Whole Foods Market Healthy Eating initiative. Goal was to transform our existing text based meal plans to not only something that was more aesethically pleasing but also easier to use, browse and understand.

Slide 2. New cleaner icon for Whole Foods Market App

To freshen up the look and feel of the look of the app, we removed a lot of heavy textures and woods from the App as part of a design refresh that was first released in time for iOS 9. I was asked to design a cleaner and brighter icon for the app to go along with the new look and feel of the app. I packaged new design for possible icon sizes for iOS and Android to make a super easy hand off to our mobile developers.

Slide 3. Whole Foods Market Digital Holiday Guide

In an effort to save money by not printing a holiday gift guide as Whole Foods Market has traditionally done, the company and regions still wanted a way to be able to promote holiday gifts and partnerships that would be available in store for the holidays. I was asked to come up with a concept to replace the traditional printed guide that was interactive, fun but met business objectives in promoting available gifts at Whole Foods Market stores through out all its regions. I came up with a concept similar to advent calendar where instead, each day for 30 days leading up to christmas, we would auto-publish a gift. I prototyped, designed and developed a simple and light solution that fit with the existing larger holiday guide that was already in place.
