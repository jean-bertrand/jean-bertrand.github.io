Cacao & Cardamom

e-commerce design for a luxury chocolatier

Client wanted her website to be a digital representation of her physical store in downtown Houston, TX. More that just expressing the bright and vibrant colors, and communicating that these were indeed luxury chocolates, we needed to design the site around making it seamless and pleasant for customers to explore and purchase chocolates for themselves or as gifts.
