Computer Aided Dispatch Concept

Concept design for computer aided dispatch emergency systems

Systems that we rely on everyday to help emergency services is highly outdated. At the center of those systems is computer aided dispatch emergency system - the system responsible for routing emergency respondes to calls for service. It is utilized for law enforcement, fire and medical emergencies. This was an attempt at simplifying the interface that emergency dispatchers work with on daily basis.
