About Me

I'm a socially conscious dreamer and currently a designer & developer for @Aceable, previously at @WholeFoods and living in beautiful Austin, TX. I'm originally from Rwanda in central Africa, I moved to the United States when I was 11 and grew up in Buffalo, NY after which I then attended Boston University to study Environmental Science & Policy.

Passion, drive, empathy and thoughtfulness are virtues I practice relentlessly in my life and work as a designer and developer. My particular areas of interest are around UX/UI and product design.
